Buddy Bag

Helping Amputees with Life on the Go

Co-Founder / Product / Manufacturing / Distribution / Strategy

Background Information

Everyday millions of people with blindness and low vision are unable to locate personal items, food, clothing, appliances and much more due to non-tactile labels.

Life as an amputee is challenging, I wanted to change this by creating the 1st prosthetic carrying case to ease the healing process.
